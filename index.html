About Me

Hello there! I’m Mark Joshwel. Games and technology have been with me since Kindergarten, with Minecraft being my introduction to games in general.

I picked up Python, my first programming language, during the April 2020 Circuit Breaker. Since then, I have created numerous projects with it, most of which are publicly available as open-source projects on my GitHub profile . I have also explored other programming languages, such as Rust and Zig, in an attempt to go “lower-level”.


My Favourite Games/Franchises:

  • Portal
  • SCP: Containment Breach
  • osu!
  • Five Nights at Freddy’s
  • The Stanley Parable


I’ve adored Portal and SCP: Containment Breach for the environments they immerse players in. Portal’s clinical and eerie levels and SCP: Containment Breaches’ massive, sci-fi-esque complex has always made me want to recreate something of the sort to this day.

My first few games were Five Nights at Freddy’s fan games, made in Clickteam Fusion 2.5 during my time in Primary School. This period of mine where I actively made games ended after a year or two alongside my time in game development.

However, with my proficiency in Python and a rekindled passion, I have decided to step into game development once more. This time, professionally.
